Wilson Plaza Brick Sale! - Place Your Order Today!

Show your support with a gift to the Wilson Stadium Plaza Bricks campaign. In appreciation for your gift, Wilson Fields Improvement Project will install a brick engraved with your personalized message at the Wilson Plaza in the Wilson High School stadium.

The new plaza will be a brick section in the corner of the new turf field. Put the name of your favorite alumnus or someone special on a brick. All proceeds from the brick sale will be used for the Wilson Fields Improvement Project. Join our community in helping us build a new turf field in Wilson Stadium.

Options for Wilson Stadium Plaza Bricks:

8"x4" Brick

Standard brick with 1-3 lines of customized text - $100 Gift

8"x8" Brick

Square brick with up to 6 lines of customized text - $250 Gift

Wilson Plaza Bricks Gift Certificates

Gift certificates allow you to reserve a brick as a gift for someone while providing them the opportunity to customize the engraved message themselves. Gift certificates are available for purchase through our online ordering system- PayPal and will be mailed to you.
